## Competitive Analysis

### Bobby App (iOS)
- **Template Count:** ~100-120 (estimated, not publicly documented)
- **Strengths:** Established iOS user base, simple interface
- **Weaknesses:** Broken notifications, no Android support, developer inactive
- **CustomSubs Advantage:** +140-160 more templates, active development

### Rocket Money (formerly Truebill)
- **Template Detection:** Automatic via bank connection
- **Coverage:** Universal (any recurring charge)
- **Weaknesses:** Requires bank linking, privacy concerns
- **CustomSubs Advantage:** Privacy-first, no bank connection, pre-filled data
